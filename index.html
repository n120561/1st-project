Liquid Helium &
Liquid Nitrogen
Cryogenic Facility

Low temperature facility (LTF) of University of Hyderabad, (UOH) Hyderabad, India, has been operating the cryogenic plants for more than 16 years. Low Temperature Facility provides liquid helium, liquid nitrogen and cryogenic support services to various facilities, and laboratories of the institute. The primary function of LTF is to meet the cryogen demands of various research facilities of the institute
